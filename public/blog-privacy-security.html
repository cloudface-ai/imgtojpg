How We Protect Your Images and Personal Data

Vinod Kumar

Published: January 27, 2025

At imgtojpg.org, your privacy and security are our top priorities. We understand that you're trusting us with your personal photos and images, and we take that responsibility seriously. This article explains exactly how we protect your data and ensure your privacy.

🕐 Files Deleted in 15 Minutes

One of our most important privacy features is our automatic file deletion system. Here's exactly how it works:

  • Automatic Cleanup: All uploaded files are automatically deleted within 15 minutes of upload
  • Background Process: Our cleanup service runs every 5 minutes to ensure timely deletion
  • No Manual Intervention: Files are deleted automatically, regardless of whether you download the converted images
  • Complete Removal: Both original and converted files are permanently removed from our servers
  • Logged Cleanup: Deletion activity is logged to the server console for operational monitoring

🛡️ Your Files Are Never Stored Permanently

Unlike many other image converters, we don't keep your images in our database or cloud storage. Once the 15-minute window passes, your files are gone forever from our servers.

🔐 HTTPS Encryption

All data transmission to and from imgtojpg.org is protected by industry-standard encryption:

  • TLS 1.2+: We use the latest TLS encryption protocols to secure all connections
  • End-to-End Security: Your files are encrypted from the moment they leave your device until they're processed
  • Certificate Validation: Our SSL certificates are automatically validated and renewed
  • HSTS Headers: We force HTTPS connections to prevent any potential downgrade attacks
  • Secure Headers: Additional security headers protect against various attack vectors

📋 GDPR Compliance

We fully comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other privacy laws:

  • Data Minimization: We only collect and process the minimum data necessary for our service
  • No Personal Data Storage: We don't store personal information beyond what's necessary for authentication
  • Right to Deletion: Users can request complete data deletion at any time
  • Transparent Processing: We clearly explain how and why we process your data
  • Regular Audits: Our privacy practices are regularly reviewed and updated

🔍 How Our File Cleanup System Works

Our automated cleanup system is designed to be both efficient and reliable:

  • Recursive Cleanup: Removes both files and empty directories
  • Error Handling: Gracefully handles any cleanup errors without affecting service
  • Monitoring: All cleanup activities are logged for transparency
