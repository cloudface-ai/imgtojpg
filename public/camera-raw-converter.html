Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our RAW photo conversion service

What RAW formats do you support?

We support all major RAW formats including Canon (CR2, CR3), Nikon (NEF, NRW), Sony (ARW, SRF, SR2), Fujifilm (RAF), Olympus (ORF), Pentax (PEF), Panasonic (RW2), Hasselblad (3FR, RDC), Phase One (IIQ), and many more professional camera formats.

Is RAW conversion really free?

Yes, our RAW conversion service is completely free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or registration required. Professional photographers deserve access to quality conversion tools without breaking the bank.

How do you preserve image quality during conversion?

We use advanced ImageMagick and LibRaw technology to ensure maximum quality preservation. Our conversion process maintains color accuracy, dynamic range, and detail while optimizing for your chosen output format.

Can I convert multiple RAW files at once?

Absolutely! Our batch processing feature allows you to convert up to 10 RAW files simultaneously. Simply select multiple files and they'll all be converted to your chosen format in one go.

What's the maximum file size for RAW conversion?

We support RAW files up to 500MB, making it perfect for high-resolution professional photography. This covers most modern camera sensors including medium format and high-megapixel cameras.

Which output formats are best for different uses?

JPG is perfect for web, social media, and general use. TIFF is ideal for printing and professional editing. PSD is best for advanced Photoshop work with layers and effects. Choose based on your specific needs.

Do you support all camera brands?

Yes, we support all major camera brands including Canon, Nikon, Sony, Fujifilm, Olympus, Pentax, Panasonic, Hasselblad, Phase One, and many others. Our service works with professional, enthusiast, and entry-level cameras.

How secure is my RAW photo data?

Your privacy is our priority. All RAW photos are processed locally and automatically deleted after conversion. We don't store, share, or access your files beyond the conversion process.

Can I use this for professional photography work?

Absolutely! Many professional photographers use our service for client work, stock photography, and commercial projects. Our conversion quality meets professional standards and is trusted by photographers worldwide.

What if my RAW file is corrupted or unsupported?

If a RAW file can't be processed, we'll show you a clear error message. This usually happens with very old camera formats or corrupted files. Most modern RAW formats from the last 15+ years are fully supported.
