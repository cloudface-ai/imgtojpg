Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: September 19, 2024

1. Subscription Cancellation

1.1 How to Cancel

  • Log into your account dashboard at imgtojpg.org/dashboard
  • Click "Manage Billing" to access your subscription settings
  • Select "Cancel Subscription" and follow the prompts
  • You can also email us at support@imgtojpg.org

1.2 When Cancellation Takes Effect

  • Immediate Cancellation: Access ends immediately, no refund for remaining period
  • End of Period: Access continues until your billing period ends (recommended)
  • No Questions Asked: We don't require a reason for cancellation

2. Refund Policy

2.1 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

  • New Subscriptions: Full refund within 7 days of first payment
  • Automatic Processing: Refunds processed within 3-5 business days
  • Original Payment Method: Refund issued to your original payment method

2.2 Prorated Refunds

  • Plan Downgrades: Credit applied to next billing cycle
  • Service Issues: Prorated refund for downtime exceeding 24 hours
  • Billing Errors: Full correction and refund of incorrect charges

2.3 Non-Refundable Situations

  • Partial month usage after 7-day guarantee period
  • Violation of Terms of Service
  • Abuse of service or quota limits
  • Chargebacks (may result in account suspension)

3. Refund Process

3.1 How to Request a Refund

  1. Email us at support@imgtojpg.org
  2. Include your account email and reason for refund
  3. We'll respond within 24 hours
  4. Refund processed within 3-5 business days

3.2 Refund Timeline

  • Credit Cards: 3-5 business days
  • UPI/NetBanking: 5-7 business days
  • International Cards: 5-10 business days
  • Bank Processing: May take additional time based on your bank

4. Plan Changes

4.1 Upgrades

  • Immediate Access: Higher limits available immediately
  • Prorated Billing: Pay difference for remaining period
  • Next Billing Cycle: Full new plan amount

4.2 Downgrades

  • End of Period: Downgrade takes effect at next billing cycle
  • Credit Applied: Unused amount credited to account
  • Quota Adjustment: Limits adjusted immediately

5. Free Plan

  • Always Available: You can always return to the free plan
  • No Cancellation Needed: Just let your subscription expire
  • Data Retention: Account data preserved for 90 days

6. Special Circumstances

6.1 Service Interruptions

  • Refund or service credit for outages exceeding 24 hours
  • Automatic credit for documented service issues
  • No refund for scheduled maintenance (announced in advance)

6.2 Technical Issues

  • Refund for conversion failures due to our service
  • No refund for user error or incompatible files
  • Support provided to resolve technical issues first

7. Contact for Refunds

For all cancellation and refund requests:

  • Email: support@imgtojpg.org
  • Subject: "Cancellation Request" or "Refund Request"
  • Include: Account email, reason, and any relevant details
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours

8. Fair Use Policy

We reserve the right to refuse refunds for:

  • Excessive refund requests (abuse of policy)
  • Violation of Terms of Service
  • Fraudulent activity or chargebacks
  • Use of service beyond reasonable limits

Need Help?

Before requesting a refund, our support team is happy to help resolve any issues you're experiencing. Contact us at support@imgtojpg.org and we'll do our best to make things right.
