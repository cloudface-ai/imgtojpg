Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 19, 2024

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Images and Files

  • Images you upload for conversion
  • File metadata (name, size, format, creation date)
  • Conversion preferences and settings

1.2 Account Information

  • Email address and display name (via Firebase Auth)
  • Authentication provider (Google, email/password)
  • Subscription plan and billing information
  • Usage statistics (conversions, file sizes, dates)

1.3 Technical Information

  • IP address and general location (for payment provider selection)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Usage patterns and performance metrics
  • Error logs and debugging information

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • Process and convert your images
  • Manage your account and subscriptions
  • Track usage against plan limits
  • Provide customer support

2.2 Billing and Payments

  • Process subscription payments
  • Generate invoices and receipts
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with tax and financial regulations

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve performance
  • Debug and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features and formats

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Image Storage

  • Temporary Storage: Images are stored only during conversion
  • Automatic Deletion: All files are deleted within 24 hours
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not keep copies of your images
  • Secure Processing: Files are processed in isolated environments

3.2 Account Data

  • Stored in AWS RDS PostgreSQL with encryption at rest
  • Authentication handled by Firebase Auth
  • Payment data processed by Razorpay (PCI compliant)
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information except:

  • Payment Processors: Razorpay for payment processing
  • Authentication: Firebase Auth for secure login
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order
  • Service Providers: AWS for hosting (with strict data protection agreements)

5. Your Rights

5.1 Access and Control

  • View your account data and usage statistics
  • Update your profile information
  • Download your data (account information)
  • Delete your account and associated data

5.2 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification (correction)
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

5.3 CCPA Rights (California Users)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination

6. Cookies and Tracking

  • Essential Cookies: For authentication and session management
  • Analytics: Google Analytics for usage statistics (anonymized)
  • No Advertising: We don't use tracking for advertising purposes
  • Your Choice: You can disable cookies in your browser

7. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in:

  • European Union (AWS eu-north-1 region)
  • United States (Firebase Auth)
  • India (Razorpay for Indian customers)

All transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and include appropriate safeguards.

8. Data Retention

  • Images: Deleted within 24 hours
  • Account Data: Retained while account is active
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 2 years for billing and analytics
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years for legal compliance
  • Deleted Accounts: Data anonymized after 30 days

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Rate limiting and abuse protection
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure file processing in isolated environments
  • Employee access controls and training

11. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Auth: User authentication and management
  • Razorpay: Payment processing (UPI, NetBanking, wallets, and cards)
  • AWS: Cloud hosting and storage
  • Google Analytics: Website analytics (anonymized)

12. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: support@imgtojpg.org
  • Subject: "Privacy Request"
  • Response time: Within 30 days

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes via email or website notice.

Questions? If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please don't hesitate to contact us at support@imgtojpg.org.
