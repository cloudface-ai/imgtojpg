Event Photo Sharing Without App Downloads

Give guests instant access via QR or link—simple, private, and fast.

Guests shouldn’t have to install an app to view photos. With imgtojpg.org, sharing is as simple as scanning a QR code or opening a private link—works on every device, instantly.

To keep viewing snappy, convert heavy sources to web‑ready formats beforehand. Use HEIC to JPG Converter for iPhone photos, PNG to JPG Converter when shrinking graphics for galleries, and RAW conversion for pro shoots. For deeper background on modern image delivery, see web.dev guidance on image performance, MDN’s image types, and Cloudflare on image formats.

How it works in 3 steps

  1. Create a collection: Upload and group photos by event.
  2. Generate access: Print a QR for the venue or share unique links.
  3. Instant viewing: Guests open in their browser—no installs, no logins.

Privacy, your way

  • Public collections for open events.
  • Invite-only links for private groups.
  • Per-guest links when you need granular control.

Why this beats “app-required” platforms

  • Zero friction: Everyone knows how to scan a QR.
  • No app fatigue: No storage, permissions, or updates.
  • Faster support: Works on iOS, Android, desktop—right away.

Learn and plan your rollout

Start small with an open collection, then test invite‑only links for family galleries or VIP previews. Share a single clear CTA and pin a QR at the venue.
